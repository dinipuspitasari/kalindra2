ABOUT US

PT. KALIANDRA SETYATAMA is Trading and Engineering Company was founded in 1992. and has its office at Jl. Merawan, Cinere One Commercial Park Blok KA-1, Pangkalan Jati, Cinere Depok – 16513 As a Representative and Sole distributor of electrical product, the main activity of the company is as a supplier of the product to the main contractors.

CUSTOMER

The Customers of PT. KALIANDRA SETYATAMA are from Electrical Utility Company (PLN), Power Plant, Sub Station, Oil and Gas Industries, Panel’s Makers. Petrochemical Industries, Mechanical and Electrical Contractors, Pulp Industries And General Industries.
